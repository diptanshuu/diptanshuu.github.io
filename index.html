Who are we?
Founded in 2021, by Diptanshu Mahish, Peace Tempo Records is a record label based in India .
We focus mainly on the softer and lesser known genres , such as Lo-Fi, Orchestral , Ambient, Indie and many more such gneres. Our mission is to provide upliftment , and growth to all artists , especially those who need it more :)
